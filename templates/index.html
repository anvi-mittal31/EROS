Let's start with a small gist of the project:

SIGN UP PAGE:
Here the user can sign up as per the form given below and the user will be able to go into the depth of the project via using the hyperlinks in the navbar upadated on the sceen, i.e., About, and Contact tabs. From here, the user will be taken to the vedio feed screen.

EMOTION DETECTION:
Here the user uses the vedio feed screen to capture the vedio of the person and thereby, the emotion will be detected as per the emotion dispalyed on the face of th person and the user can proceed to the next screen via the button provided.

PREDICTION VALUE:
Here the user can get the predicted value as per the vedio feed and the user gets an option here to click on the screen and get the mood-wise wallpapers as per their mood detected. Here, the user gets an option to change their wallpapers as per their mood, or else they can go for opposite end of their mood wallpapers, which can be completely upto their choice.

WALLPAPER APPLICATION:
Here the user can get a variety of wallpapers to dive into according to their moods, and download the wallpapers by clicking on the wallpapers they like and the wallpaper will be downloaded on one-click. The wallpapers will be suggested as per the mood button selected by the user, making the inteface, user friendly.
